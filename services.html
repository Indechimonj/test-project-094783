Lawn Care

This service encompasses a comprehensive range of activities designed to ensure your lawn remains healthy, lush, and aesthetically pleasing throughout the year. This includes regular mowing, edging, and trimming to maintain a neat and tidy appearance. Fertilization and weed control treatments are applied to promote robust grass growth and prevent invasive species from taking over. Aeration and overseeding are performed to enhance soil health and ensure even grass coverage.

Hardscaping

Hardscaping also encompasses the creation of outdoor living spaces, such as fire pits, seating areas, and pergolas, which provide structure and focal points in the landscape. Sustainable Landscaping Solutions

This service focuses on using native and drought-resistant plants that require minimal water and maintenance, reducing the environmental footprint. Smoothscape implements eco-friendly irrigation systems, such as drip irrigation, to conserve water and ensure optimal plant hydration. Soil health is enhanced through organic fertilizers and composting, eliminating the need for harmful chemicals. Additionally, sustainable practices include rainwater harvesting, permeable paving to reduce runoff, and the incorporation of natural habitats to support local wildlife.
